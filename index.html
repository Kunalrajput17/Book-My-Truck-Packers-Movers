Official Terms & Conditions

1. Quotation Validity: Quotes are valid for 15 days from the date of issue.

2. Insurance Claims: Claims must be reported with visual evidence before the transport vehicle leaves the destination premises. Deductibles apply.

3. Prohibited Goods: Book My Truck strictly prohibits the transport of inflammable liquids, gas cylinders, perishable foods, cash, and high-value jewelry.

4. Payments: 50% advance payment is required upon packing completion, and the remaining 50% must be cleared prior to unloading.
