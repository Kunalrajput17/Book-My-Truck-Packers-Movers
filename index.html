Official Terms & Conditions

1. Quotation Validity: Quotes are valid for 15 days from the date of issue.

3. Prohibited Goods: Book My Truck strictly prohibits the transport of inflammable liquids, gas cylinders, perishable foods, cash, and high-value jewelry.
