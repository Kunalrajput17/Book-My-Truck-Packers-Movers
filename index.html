Frequently Asked

Is my luggage insured during transit?

Yes, we offer comprehensive transit insurance at a nominal 3% of declared value to protect against any unforeseen circumstances.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 7-10 days in advance for inter-city moves to secure your preferred date and fleet.

Are there any hidden charges?

Absolutely not. The quotation provided after the initial survey is final. Tolls and taxes are clearly mentioned upfront.
